The Key

A short story about a girl, Amber Collins, who has a magic key that can open any door and create a portal between doors. With such a valuable key, she and anyone she bumps into becomes a target. James Rook, an old school friend, was unlucky to come across the mysterious girl one night. The Dark Men, a mysterious league of assassins, soon comes after James Rook in the hopes that he will lead them to Amber and the key. Together James and Amber must destroy the key before it falls into the wrong hands.

The short story is available on the kindle via Amazon and other e-readers such as the Nook, Sony Reader, etc. via Smashwords.
